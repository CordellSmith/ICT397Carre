Denotes pure height-map terrain data. Pure terrains usually consist of quads, sometimes triangles, in a regular grid. The x,y coordinates of all vertex positions refer to the x,y coordinates on the terrain height map, the z-axis stores the elevation at a specific point.

TER (Terragen) and HMP (3D Game Studio) are height map formats. \begin{Desc}
\item[Note:]\hyperlink{namespace_assimp}{Assimp} is probably not the best choice for loading $\ast$huge$\ast$ terrains - fully triangulated data takes extremely much free store and should be avoided as long as possible (typically you'll do the triangulation when you actually need to render it). \end{Desc}
